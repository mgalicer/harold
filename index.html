SeaKay Inc, a California public benefit corporation, was founded upon the vision that the not for profit sector could work better for everyone.

SeaKay employs a team of nonprofit professionals who lead their field to provide community networking, management consulting, financial management, and marketing and fundraising.

Digital Opportunity

While digital opportunity focuses upon those in underserved populations who have not yet discovered the full range of possibilities from new technologies, it extends beyond this point. Digital opportunity also benefits new and emerging businesses, government entities seeking greater connection to those they inform and serve, and public safety and educational entities seeking to extend their reach and eliminate barriers to effectiveness. The Metro Connect network provides a basis for productive interaction for the entire community, offering a greater level of inclusion to all participating constituencies.

device_hub

Broadband Networks

The network is a catalyst to economic development. Ubiquitous coverage by the network will provide widespread opportunity to the residents, government and businesses. Additionally, it will enable emerging technologies to support development in underserved areas seeking participation in the digital economy. The network is economically viable, built upon a collaborative sponsorship model that seeks efficient and effective achievement of objectives and promotes multiple and diverse sources of revenue.

Non-Profit Management Services

SeaKay works with nonprofit organizations to make the most of their administrative resources. SeaKay supports the development of realistic programmatic and organizational development goals, implementing these goals through trainings, coaching and IT support. SeaKay assists in the creation of evaluation criteria to accurately measure progress toward achieving the goals.
