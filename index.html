The vision: expanding digital opportunity

SeaKay Inc, a California public benefit corporation, was founded upon the vision that the not for profit sector could work better for everyone.

SeaKay´s vision for ubiquitous broadband networks supported by service learning programs are the basis for a region´s pursuit of the rewards of the 21st century economy. Economic growth and renewal is brought about by a higher degree of connectivity – to resources, knowledge and capital. The vast resources that this network brings create what SeaKay calls Digital Opportunity.

accessibility

Digital Opportunity

While digital opportunity focuses upon those in underserved populations who have not yet discovered the full range of possibilities from new technologies, it extends beyond this point. Digital opportunity also benefits new and emerging businesses, government entities seeking greater connection to those they inform and serve, and public safety and educational entities seeking to extend their reach and eliminate barriers to effectiveness. The Metro Connect network provides a basis for productive interaction for the entire community, offering a greater level of inclusion to all participating constituencies.

Broadband Networks

The network is a catalyst to economic development. Ubiquitous coverage by the network will provide widespread opportunity to the residents, government and businesses. Additionally, it will enable emerging technologies to support development in underserved areas seeking participation in the digital economy. The network is economically viable, built upon a collaborative sponsorship model that seeks efficient and effective achievement of objectives and promotes multiple and diverse sources of revenue.

Non-Profit Support Services

SeaKay uses a technology toolset and broadband networks to enable nonprofit organizations to make the most of their administrative resources. Broadband networks enable organizations to provide a wide range of services and connectivity to the Organization’s constituents. SeaKay supports the development of automation and communication technologies that increase efficiencies and enable staff to focus on program service delivery. The processes are straightforward and can be used by all levels of the organization´s staff and volunteer leaders.

Seakay Broadband

Seakay Broadband is a deployment of cost effective technologies that supports provides high-speed fixed wireless internet service to approximately 600 square miles in underserved communities from Westport on the Mendocino Coast to the Blue Lakes area in in Lake County in California.
