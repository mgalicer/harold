Digital Opportunity

While digital opportunity focuses upon those in underserved populations who have not yet discovered the full range of possibilities from new technologies, it extends beyond this point. Digital opportunity also benefits new and emerging businesses, government entities seeking greater connection to those they inform and serve, and public safety and educational entities seeking to extend their reach and eliminate barriers to effectiveness. The Metro Connect network provides a basis for productive interaction for the entire community, offering a greater level of inclusion to all participating constituencies.

Broadband Networks

The network is a catalyst to economic development. Ubiquitous coverage by the network will provide widespread opportunity to the residents, government and businesses. Additionally, it will enable emerging technologies to support development in underserved areas seeking participation in the digital economy. The network is economically viable, built upon a collaborative sponsorship model that seeks efficient and effective achievement of objectives and promotes multiple and diverse sources of revenue.

Non-Profit Management Services

SeaKay works with nonprofit organizations to make the most of their administrative resources. SeaKay supports the development of realistic programmatic and organizational development goals, implementing these goals through trainings, coaching and IT support. SeaKay assists in the creation of evaluation criteria to accurately measure progress toward achieving the goals. The processes are straightforward and can be used by all levels of the organization´s staff as well as shared with Board and volunteer leaders

About Seakay

SeaKay´s mission is to support the nonprofit sector in its efforts to work more effectively and make a significant contribution to social progress and economic development both nationally and internationally.

By taking advantage of technology and proven best practices, SeaKay employs a team of nonprofit professionals who lead their field in management consulting, hardware and software systems implementation, financial and fundraising program development and evaluation. Among the services we provide are:


Community Networking:
• Creation of community networking solutions for broadband access and public service applications. Read our brochure.
Financial Management:
• Systems development and implementation, Sarbanes-Oaxley compliance, translating financial information into useful and understandable tools for board, staff and volunteer leaders.
Management Consulting:
• Goal setting, implementation and evaluation systems, Board of Directors Development and executive coaching.
Marketing and Fundraising:
• Message development, creation and implementation of fundraising plans and individual vehicles such as direct mail, major gifts and foundation proposals.
